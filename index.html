HOW IT WORKS

SIGN UP

Sign up below. We'll match you with a team if you sign up alone, or feel free to come with a team already made! Just let us know. We'll take care of the rest. Any skills are welcome, we'll teach you everything you need to know.

LEARN

Friday night, sit through a series of crash courses on "how to hack hackathons" that will bring you up to speed on how to build a web-app from scratch. We'll move through start to finish, and touch on version control, deploying and best practices as well.

GET MENTORED

Completely lost and not sure what to do next? Stuck on a bug for hours? Don't worry, we've all been there and we've got you covered. After the tutorials, you'll be matched with a mentor whose job it is to help you whenever you need it.

ENJOY

Hackathons are fun! There's free food, new friends to make, internship opportunities galore, and are an awesome opportuity to learn really, really cool stuff (trust us). Do it all in a pressure-free environment and relax.

WHAT IS HACKING ANYWAYS?
AND WHAT THE HELL IS PENNAPPS?

The word hacking is vague on purpose - so as lame as it sounds - it's whatever you really want it to be. Wikipedia says the word hack refers to "the sense of playful, exploratory programming" which sounds about right. It's playing around with code - often, not really sure what you're doing, and putting things together.

PennApps is a huge (and in my opinion, the best) student hackathon, where students from all over the country get together to'hack on' a whole slew of different projects of their choice. From GIF makers to singing toilets, PennApps has done it all. If there's one hackathon you want to go to - it's this one. Take our word for it.

WHY SHOULD I DO THIS AGAIN?

A whole slew of reasons - if you want to learn how to get really good at programming, really really fast - do it a hackathon. But hackathons are really intimidating. With this, we'll take away the scary parts and leave the awesome parts. On a professional level, learning how to build apps is a really important skill today - especially if you're eyeing companies like Google or Facebook. Mostly though, hacking is a lot of fun and this is a great opportunity to meet new people.

WHAT IF I DON'T KNOW ANYTHING?

We were all there and everyone has to start from somewhere. We'll teach you everything you have to know. For the people who already have some but not all chops - you can pick and choose which tutorials to go to and we'll cater to your needs.
